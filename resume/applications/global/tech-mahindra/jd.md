Last updated: 2026-04-06

## Company

Tech Mahindra

## Role

Key coordinator for gaming industry engagements in Korea

## About the Role

As a key coordinator for gaming industry engagements in Korea, this role combines technical expertise, client coordination, and event execution. You’ll support high-value meetings, interpret technical content, and help drive successful partnerships with leading game studios.

## Responsibilities

- Coordinate meetings and events with major gaming clients and partners.
- Provide Korean-English consecutive interpretation for technical presentations and live demos.
- Manage customer support issues and ensure clear communication across teams.
- Advise partners on agreements, licensing, and program inquiries.
- Participate in weekly sales and account-planning discussions.
- Support event logistics, schedules, and on-site coordination for technical roadshows.
- Prepare technical summaries, follow-up materials, and customer insights for internal teams.
- Maintain strong relationships with studios, publishers, and ecosystem partners.

## Requirements

- Fluent in Korean and English.
- Experience in technical account management, and event coordination.
- Understanding of game asset production workflows.
- Knowledge of data structures for meshes, materials, and texture data.
- Familiarity of real-time computer graphics optimization techniques.
- Experience with modern game engines is a plus.
- Strong organizational and communication skills.
- Ability to work cross-functionally in fast-moving environments.

The company is part of the Mahindra Group, a USD 19.4 billion conglomerate, and is recognized as one of the top IT service providers in the world.
